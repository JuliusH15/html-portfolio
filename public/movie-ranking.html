The Best Movies According To Julius

My top 3 favorite movies of all time

The Matrix

Excellent combination of stylish action, philosophy and sci fi. I don't think any movie will ever replace this as number 1.


John Wick Chapter 4

I loved the fight choreography, the world building and thought the movie had good pacing despite being 3 hours long.


Inception

A very good complex thought provoking sci fi film.
